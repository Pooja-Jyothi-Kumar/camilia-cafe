ABOUT US

Welcome to CAMILIA!

At CAMILIA , we take pride in bringing the rich and authentic flavors of Turkish Shawarma to your plate. Our journey began with a passion for preserving the culinary heritage of Turkey and sharing it with the world.
Inspired by the bustling streets of Istanbul and the aromas that waft through its vibrant markets, we embarked on a mission to recreate the magic of Turkish Shawarma right here for you. Our founders, who have a deep connection to Turkish culture and cuisine, envisioned a place where every bite would transport you to the heart of Turkey.
